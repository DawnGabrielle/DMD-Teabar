About Us

DMD Teabar's name is inspired by the initials of its owners Darrylner and Michael Dave. It was founded last November 2021 by Mrs. Darrylner Kate San Antonio. In Morong, Rizal, her husband operates a perfumery shop. Next to it, there used to be a milktea shop, but it has since been permanently closed. People frequently return to that spot to look for the milktea shop, so Mrs. San Antonio thought of opening a comparable type of business there.

Specialty

We are DMD Teabar, a modest yet dedicated business specializing in milk tea, ice scramble, and coffee. We are passionate about providing high-quality products and first-rate services which is why we pledge to provide you with the best of both worlds. If you are seeking something refreshing, you're in the right place! We work hard to be diligent and creative in providing our customers with what they want and prioritizing their desires above all else.

Goal

We strive to provide the highest quality and most interesting variety of drinks, and snacks to everyone at all times. We endeavor to treat our customers as if they were guests, and we intend to provide prompt, efficient, courteous, and relaxing service. We shall always maintain a clean and appealing place and provide a pleasant, friendly environment. We also hope to open branches in nearby towns and expand our business in the near future.
